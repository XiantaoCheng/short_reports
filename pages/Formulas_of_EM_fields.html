Formulas of EM fields

Potential fields,
$$ \phi \left( t,\vec{r} \right)=\frac{1}{4 \pi \epsilon_{0}} \frac{-e}{\left(1-\hat{R} \left( t_{ret} \right)\cdot \vec{\beta} \left( t_{ret} \right)\right) \left|\vec{r}-\vec{r}_{0} \left( t_{ret} \right)\right|} \tag{1} $$ $$ \vec{A} \left( t,\vec{r} \right)=\frac{\mu_{0}}{4 \pi} \frac{-e \vec{v} \left( t_{ret} \right)}{\left(1-\hat{R} \left( t_{ret} \right)\cdot \vec{\beta} \left( t_{ret} \right)\right) \left|\vec{r}-\vec{r}_{0} \left( t_{ret} \right)\right|} \tag{2} $$ EM fields,
$$ \vec{E}=\frac{-e}{4 \pi \epsilon_{0}} \frac{\hat{R}-\vec{\beta}}{\gamma^{2} \left(1-\hat{R}\cdot \vec{\beta}\right)^{3} R^{2}}+\frac{-e/c}{4 \pi \epsilon_{0}} \frac{\hat{R}\times \left(\left(\hat{R}-\vec{\beta}\right)\times \frac{d\vec{\beta}}{dt}\right)}{\left(1-\hat{R}\cdot \vec{\beta}\right)^{3} R} \tag{3} $$ $$ \vec{B}=\hat{R}\times \vec{E} \tag{4} $$
