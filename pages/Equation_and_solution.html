Equation and solution

The transport equation is,
$$ \frac{\partial}{\partial z}\left(f \left( \theta,z \right)\right)=-N f \left( \theta,z \right) \int_{}^{}{d\chi\ }{\sigma \left( \chi \right) sin \left( \chi \right)}+N \int_{}^{}{d\chi\ }{f \left( \theta-\chi,z \right) \sigma \left( \chi \right) sin \left( \chi \right)} \tag{1} $$ We can take the approximation, \(sin(\chi)\approx \chi\), which will make eqn.(1) become,
$$ \frac{\partial}{\partial z}\left(f \left( \theta,z \right)\right)=-N f \left( \theta,z \right) \int_{}^{}{d\chi\ }{\sigma \left( \chi \right) \chi}+N \int_{}^{}{d\chi\ }{f \left( \theta-\chi,z \right) \sigma \left( \chi \right) \chi} \tag{2} $$ In the case of Coulomb scattering, the differential cross section is,
$$ \sigma \left( \chi \right)\approx \frac{Z^{2} e^{4}}{16 \pi^{2} \epsilon_{0}^{2} E_{0}^{2}} \frac{1}{\chi^{4}} \tag{3} $$ The solution is,
$$ g \left( \eta,z \right)=e^{-\Omega \left( \eta \right) z+\Omega_{0}} \tag{4} $$ $$ f \left( \theta,z \right)=\int_{}^{}{d\eta\ }{g \left( \eta,z \right) e^{i \eta \theta}} \tag{5} $$ $$ \Omega \left( \eta \right)\approx \frac{N Z^{2} e^{4}}{8 \pi^{2} \epsilon_{0}^{2} E_{0}^{2}} \left(\frac{3}{4} \eta^{2}+\frac{\eta^{2}}{2} Ci \left( \eta \chi_{0} \right)+\frac{i \eta}{\chi_{0}}-\frac{i \eta^{2} \pi}{4}\right) \tag{6} $$ $$ \chi_{0}=\frac{Z e^{2}}{4 \pi \epsilon_{0}} \frac{1}{E_{0}} \frac{1}{r_{0}} \tag{7} $$ $$ Ci \left( x \right)=\gamma+ln \left( x \right)+\sum_{n=1}^{\infty}{\frac{\left(-1\right)^{n} x^{2 n}}{2 n \Gamma \left( 2 n+1 \right)}} \tag{8} $$ where \(\gamma\approx 0.5772156649...\), which is Euler-Mascheroni constant.
It is not a Gaussian function, but it is closed to,
$$ f \left( \theta \right)=A exp \left( -\frac{\theta^{2}}{\Delta \theta^{2}} \right) \tag{9} $$ $$ \Delta \theta=2 \sqrt{\frac{b^{2}+c^{2}}{b}} \tag{10} $$ $$ b=\frac{N Z^{2} e^{4}}{8 \pi^{2} \epsilon_{0}^{2} E_{0}^{2}} \left(\frac{3}{4}+\frac{1}{2} Ci \left( \eta \chi_{0} \right)\right) z\approx \frac{N Z^{2} e^{4}}{8 \pi^{2} \epsilon_{0}^{2} E_{0}^{2}} \left(\frac{3}{4}+\frac{1}{2} Ci \left( 0.1 \chi_{0} \right)\right) z \tag{11} $$ $$ c=\frac{\pi}{4} \frac{N Z^{2} e^{4}}{8 \pi^{2} \epsilon_{0}^{2} E_{0}^{2}} z \tag{12} $$
