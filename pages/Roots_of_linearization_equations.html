Roots of linearization equations

Equations are,
$$ \left(\omega-\omega_{1}\right) \left(\omega-\omega_{2}\right) \left(\omega-\omega_{3}\right) \left(\omega-\omega_{4}\right)=c^{2} k^{2} \omega_{p}^{2} a_{0}^{2} \tag{7} $$ where,
$$ \omega_{1,2}=\omega_{0}\pm \sqrt{\omega_{p}^{2}+c^{2} \left(k_{0}-k\right)^{2}} \tag{8} $$ $$ \omega_{3,4}=-\frac{i \nu_{e}\pm \sqrt{-\nu_{e}^{2}+4 \left(\omega_{p}^{2}+3 v_{B}^{2} k^{2}\right)}}{2} \tag{9} $$
